# Beating the Invincibles ⚽

A free browser game for friends — a soccer take on [hoopsmatic's 73-9 game](https://hoopsmatic.com/73-9-game), set in Champions League history.

**Play it:** open `index.html`, or the GitHub Pages link on this repo.

## How it works

1. You get a **€500M** transfer budget and pick a formation: **4-3-3**, **3-5-2**, or **5-2-3**.
2. The wheel lands on a random **Champions League squad** (1992 → today). Sign **one player per spin** at his market value until your XI is complete. Re-spins cost €30M; go broke and you're signing free emergency loanees.
3. Your XI plays a **home-and-away knockout tie** against a legendary giant — simulated 10,000 times with a Poisson goal model (extra time and penalties included).

Giants, easiest to hardest: Arsenal 2003-04 Invincibles → Man United 1998-99 → Man City 2022-23 → Real Madrid 2016-17 → Barcelona 2010-11.

## Notes

- One self-contained HTML file. No backend, no accounts, no tracking, no ads.
- Player ratings, prices, and season stats are the house's opinion / plausible generated numbers — argue accordingly.
- Club shirts are original SVG drawings; no official badges or player likenesses are used. Not affiliated with UEFA or any club. Non-commercial, made for fun.
